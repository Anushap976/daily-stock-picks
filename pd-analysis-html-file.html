üìö What is PD Analysis?

PD (Premium & Discount) Analysis is an advanced trading methodology from the Inner Circle Trader (ICT) that identifies optimal entry points by analyzing where institutional "smart money" is likely to enter the market. The framework divides price ranges into Premium Zones (expensive - sell areas) and Discount Zones (cheap - buy areas) using Fibonacci levels and institutional order flow patterns.

Key Concept: Buy in Discount Zones during uptrends. Sell in Premium Zones during downtrends. Always align with market structure and liquidity.

üìñ ICT PD Array Methodology

  1. Identify Market Structure
    Determine if market is in uptrend (higher highs/higher lows) or downtrend (lower highs/lower lows). Look for Break of Structure (BOS) or Market Structure Shift (MSS).
  2. Mark Dealing Range
    Draw Fibonacci from swing high to swing low. 50% level = Equilibrium. Above 50% = Premium Zone. Below 50% = Discount Zone.
  3. Identify ICT Tools in Zones
    Look for Order Blocks, Fair Value Gaps (FVG), Breaker Blocks, Mitigation Blocks, and liquidity voids in appropriate zones.
  4. Wait for Price Reaction
    In uptrend: Wait for price to retrace to Discount Zone and show bullish rejection. In downtrend: Wait for price to retrace to Premium Zone and show bearish rejection.
  5. Execute with Confirmation
    Enter when price shows engulfing pattern, liquidity sweep, or strong momentum candle in the zone. Target Premium Zone (for longs) or Discount Zone (for shorts).
